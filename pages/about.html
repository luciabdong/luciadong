Hi, I'm Lucia!

As a newly board certified music therapist, My ambition is to bring awareness to the power of music in medicine. I have recently completed my 1200-hour internship at Children’s Hospital of Orange County (CHOC), upon receiving my bachelor’s in music therapy with a minor in psychology from Berklee College of Music. I have also learned the fundamentals of music therapy at multiple establishments such as McLean Hospital, Massachusetts General Hospital (MGH), and several various programs through the Community Music Center of Boston. From these collective experiences, I am looking to further my skills in music therapy with focuses on neuroscience and clinical research.

Growing up in the Bay Area, California, I began my passion for music early in school and later devoted much of my time towards high school choir. This provided many opportunities for me to develop leadership skills and grow as a musician, earning the Director’s Award for my dedication in musicianship and service. With a family background in medicine, I have learned the importance of helping those in need. I began volunteering at multiple non-profit organizations for youth music education, as well as assisting and caregiving for those who are neurodivergent and chronically ill. Wanting to combine and share my interests, led me to a career path in music therapy.
