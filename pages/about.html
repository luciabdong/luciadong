Hi, I'm Lucia!

As an aspiring music therapist, Lucia's ambition is to bring awareness to the power of music in medicine. Her educational training has enabled her to practice clinical work at various establishments such as Massachusetts General Hospital and diverse programs through the Community Music Center of Boston. She has also volunteered at multiple non-profit organizations in the Bay Area, CA, from music education for youths to providing for those with developmental disabilities and special needs.

Growing up in the Bay Area, she began her passion for music early in school and later devoted much of her time towards her high school choir. This provided many opportunities to take leadership and grow as a musician – such as performing internationally in Vatican City at St. Peter’s Basilica, workshopping with San Francisco’s Chanticleer, as well as directing/arranging for an acapella group, earning her the director’s award for her dedication in musicianship and service.

Lucia is currently wrapping up her bachelor’s degree in music therapy with a minor in psychology at Berklee College of Music, participating as a performer in Crepusculum Choir.
