Hi, I'm Lucia!

As an aspiring music therapist, Lucia's ambition is to bring awareness to the power of music in medicine. Originally from the Bay Area, CA, she began her passion for music early in school and devoted much of her time toward her high school choir. This provided many opportunities to take leadership and grow as a musician; such as performing internationally at St. Peter’s Basilica, workshopping with San Francisco’s Chanticleer, as well as directing/arranging for an acapella group, earning her the director’s award for her dedication in musicianship and service. She additionally volunteered at community center Abilities United, aiding children and adolescents with mental and physical disabilities. Lucia is currently wrapping up her Bachelor’s degree in music therapy with a minor in psychology at Berklee College of Music while participating as an active member of Crepusculum Choir.
